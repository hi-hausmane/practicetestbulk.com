Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When you upgrade, you'll be charged a prorated amount. When you downgrade, the change takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

What happens if I exceed my question limit?

If you reach your monthly question limit on the Free or Pro plan, you'll see a message prompting you to upgrade. You can also wait until the next month when your limit resets. No overage charges—you're always in control.

Is there a free trial for Pro or Business?

The Free plan serves as your trial! Start there to test the quality and features. When you're ready for more generations or advanced features, upgrade to Pro or Business with confidence.

Do unused questions roll over to the next month?

No, unused questions don't roll over. Your question limit resets on the first day of each billing period. This keeps pricing simple and predictable.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Absolutely. You can cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period, and you won't be charged again.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) via Stripe. Your payment information is securely processed and never stored on our servers.

Who owns the generated questions?

You do! All questions generated through PracticeTestBulk are 100% yours to use commercially. Upload them to Udemy, sell them, or use them however you like—no attribution required.
